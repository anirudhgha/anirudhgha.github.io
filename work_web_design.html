007 : Designing the Headline p-bit Website

I have designed the headline website for p-bit research at purdue university, www.purdue.edu/p-bit. Feel free to check it out to learn more about my research! This website is broken into 3 parts.

  1. A one-page, modern introduction to the ideas behind p-bits
  2. A p-bit simulator built as a javascript application into the website.
  3. Instructions to access an FPGA p-computer I help build on the AWS cloud.

This work (poster below) resulted in a 1st place poster award.

  • 1st place research poster for Purdue-P, a Platform for p-bit research (out of a total of 9 posters) [Oct. 2019]
